About Me

Born in Chicago, grown in Denver, matured in Los Angeles, and now rooted in Indianapolis. My love of nature led me to Humboldt State University in Arcata, California, where I earned my bachelor's degree in Environmental Science. My love of international travel took me to many places around the globe, including a semester abroad in Xi'an, China, and 3 years in Seoul, South Korea. Blending these loves of the environment and multiculturalism took me to Los Angeles, California, where I worked in the energy efficiency industry for over a decade helping homeowners and businesses save energy and money through various utility-sponsored programs. My love of family has brought me to Indianapolis, to be near mine, and my detail-oriented, problem-solving, logical character has brought me to a new career path: software development, which I look forward to using in new ways to help protect the planet and connect people around it.
